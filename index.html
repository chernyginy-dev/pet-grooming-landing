Expert Advice

Pet Care Tips

01
🛁

Bath Frequency

Dogs benefit from a bath every 4–6 weeks. Over-washing strips natural coat oils and causes skin dryness.

02
🐾

Daily Brushing

Brush your pet daily to prevent matting. Long-coat breeds need extra attention behind the ears and legs.

03
💅

Nail Health

Nails should be trimmed monthly. Too long nails alter paw posture and cause joint pain over time.

04
🦷

Dental Care

Brush your pet's teeth 2–3 times a week with pet-safe toothpaste to prevent gum disease.
